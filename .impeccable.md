## Design Context

### Users
Corporate clients — banks, telecoms, AI/SaaS founders in Mongolia + global. Evaluating a design partner. Need to feel confident, not sold to. Visiting on desktop primarily, mid-research mode.

### Brand Personality
Precise. Warm. Capable. Premium without arrogance. Friendly but serious.

### Aesthetic Direction
80% clean white negative space / 20% gradient accent areas (hero, section breaks). Ref: newgenre.studio — heavy whitespace, friendly-premium minimalism, editorial restraint. Light mode only. Lime accent (#b8f542) as the sole color pop. No dark mode. No ornament for ornament's sake.

### Design Principles
1. **Whitespace is the design** — let content breathe, never fill for the sake of filling
2. **80/20 white-to-gradient** — gradients earn their place, not sprinkled everywhere
3. **Type does the work** — hierarchy via weight and size contrast, not color or decoration
4. **Friendly restraint** — warm enough to not feel cold, minimal enough to feel premium
5. **Every element earns its pixel** — if it doesn't help the visitor decide, remove it
